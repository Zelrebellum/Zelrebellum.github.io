Teeth: The Organ of Evolutionary Confusion

Encapsulating concepts of, Natural Selection, Specification, Co-evolution, and Pleiotropy

Focusing on mammals with inclusion of vertebrate relations.

[Sentance claim to support]


The planned format is a website focusing on the condensing and accessibility of information about the topic. Providing clear information, with supporting visuals to help comprehension of the subject matter. Ideally, I would like it to be informative, but slightly playful, to keep the reader engaged and provide additional help for comprehension. Accessibility of information and alternative learning styles is very important to me as someone who struggles with learning.

Key points of discussion are as follows:

  • Unknown Origin, but there are Theories
    • Teeth have conflicting theories of origin and are something that has confused scientists. I plan to break down researcher’s different theories in addition to some general history of teeth to use as an introduction.
  • Let’s get Down to Basics … Basic Structures
    • While structures of teeth vary from different species, there are still core structures that will be mentioned. This section will provide more visuals and definition like segments the reader can come back to if needed.
  • Tooth Complexity and its Leading to Diversification
    • Likely the largest section. This is where the majority of my information and interests were found. This is what the previous sections were preparing for. Ideally another visual heavy section, but that may be out of scope for the project.
    • Likely subsections of this section will bring in more species-specific history.
  • Interactions and Relations with other Structures
    • This section will talk about teeth in relation to mostly jaws and tastebuds. Teeth are part of a system, so talking about that relationship provides well roundedness in the information and acts as a good conclusion to wrap up the topic.
